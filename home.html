CookCountyRecorder.com Redesign

Some Context

Most of us know that government websites can be a pain to use. No seriously. So, I decided to find one of Illinois's best. I decided on my local Recorder's site, which I thought could benefit from a little boost. Here it is below, but feel free to take a look at the original page. Let's see how it goes.

Original Site Usability

Usability Problems

  • Unintuitive layout
  • Poor color contrast
  • Unneccessary top banner
  • Excess navigation options
  • Low Readability
  • Information overload
  • Conflicting Information

Accessibility Issues

Using WebAim's Wave, I was able to detect a couple of usability issues. Surprisingly, the website was responsive––it replaced its menu with a dropdown icon at smaller screen sizes. As expected, most of the issues involved contrast. Furthermore, there were a few linked images missing alt text. When it came to using screenreaders, I used Mac's Voiceover function. The reader could only detect the images and links. It did not read the paragraphs or headers.
